Madison High School Small Business Competition

Welcome to the Madison High School Small Business / Entrepreneurial Competition!

Madison High School is proud to present an exciting opportunity for budding entrepreneurs and business enthusiasts. Showcase your innovative business ideas and stand a chance to win scholarships while gaining invaluable business experience!

About the Competition

Our competition aims to inspire students to develop their entrepreneurial skills, engage with the local business community, and apply classroom knowledge to real-world challenges.

Prizes

  1. First Place: $250 scholarship + Certificate of Achievement
  2. Second Place: $250 scholarship + Certificate of Achievement

All participants will gain practical business insights and network with local business leaders.

Competition Format

  1. Submission of Business Proposals: Students are to submit a detailed business proposal. This should include an executive summary, target market, product/service description, and a basic financial plan.
  2. Presentation: Shortlisted candidates will present their ideas in front of a panel of local business leaders and educators.
  3. Feedback & Networking: Post-presentation, participants will receive feedback and have the opportunity to network with judges and local entrepreneurs.

Eligibility

  1. Open to all students of Madison High School.
  2. Participants can work solo or in teams of up to three members.
  3. The business idea must be original and not infringe on any copyrights or trademarks.

FAQs

  • Do I need a fully fleshed out business plan? No, but the more details you provide, the better your chances of being shortlisted.
  • Can I modify my proposal after submission? Modifications can be made up until the submission deadline. After that, your last submission will be considered final.
  • Who will be judging the competition? A mix of Madison High School educators and local business leaders.

For more questions, reach out to MadisonHSBusinessComp@email.com.
